About Us

AquaGrill offers globally inspired recipes in an inviting fine dining setting. Working with small farms, our menu changes constantly to showcase the finest local, sustainable, and hygiene produce at its peak. Thoughtfully blending flavors from around the world with classic techniques, we create vibrant dishes that are exciting to diners of all kinds. Our bar features inventive cocktails highlighting our house-made infusions and fresh juices, as well as alcoholic drinks and an internationally recognized wine list.

Our Story

The AquaGrill teams’ passion for cooking was initially kindled in Sainte-Anne-de-Bellevue, the establishment where they studied in a neighbourhood lying on the outskirts of Montreal. It is where they grew up, amid the hubbub of dishes, pots and pans and clients. The bar was their living room, their playground, where they did their homework, watched television... whence the aroma of the stews generously, simply and honestly prepared by their master, wafted in.
